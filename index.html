About Pamekasan

Pamekasan is a town that located on the island of Madura.

This town is famous for culinary tourism and the beauty contest for “Sape Sonok” on Madura Island. Pamekasan is called the city of Gerbang Salam as a form of developing an Islamic, civilized society, and rejecting all forms of unlawful behavior.

Culture

THE FAMOUS PAMEKASAN CULTURE

Madura is not only popular as a salt-producing region. This island, which is separated from mainland Java, has a number of tourist sites that hold interesting cultural stories. One of them, Pamekasan Regency.

Bull Race

Karapan Sapi, is Madura annual traditional event, it is such a bull racing tournament. It usually being held at week end within August to September, or ideally before or after the fasting month.

Sape Sonok

The Sonok Cow Contest is a beauty contest for a pair of female Madura cows, in which the cows are strung or flanked using a pangonong and skillfully follow the handler's instructions, then the pair of cows walk around following the rhythm of the music to go to enter a gate.

Topeng Ghettak Dance

It’s a reflection of King Baladewa’s movement with strong character, stiff, open minded and straight in revealing every problem is identical Madurese character

Rondhing Dance

A unify of drama, movement, music art and a piece of joke illustrates a story of a man who pretending to be an obedient Dutch soldier but as a matter of fact opposing the Dutch government.

One Night in Madura

Every year Madura has stunning cultural and traditional performances. Become an attractive tour package. Usually held at the end of October every year. There is a Karapan Sapi festival combined with a cultural performance entitled Overnight in Madura.

Ul-Daul

Ul-daul is a musical art whose musical instruments come from used goods, such as barrels, large jerry cans, and many more in a dynamic sound.
